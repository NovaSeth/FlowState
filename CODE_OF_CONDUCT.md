# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Flow State community a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment:

- Being kind, patient, and respectful toward other people.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Behavior that is not acceptable includes harassment of any kind, personal or
political attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and
any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
professional setting.

## Our standard in full

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1. Please read the full text, including the detailed enforcement
guidelines, here:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer. Replace this line with a real contact method
(for example, a maintainer email address or a private GitHub report) before
publishing the repository. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.
